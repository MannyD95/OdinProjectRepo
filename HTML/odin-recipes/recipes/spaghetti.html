Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

A simple and delicious Italian pasta dish that delivers a kick of garlic and red pepper flakes in every bite, perfect for a quick weeknight dinner.

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghett
  • 1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 4 garlic cloves, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • Salt, to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped
  • Optional: Grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Boil a large pot of salted water and cook the spaghetti until al dente, then drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat, then add the sliced garlic and red pepper flakes. Saut√© until the garlic turns golden, being careful not to burn it.
  3. Add the cooked spaghetti to the skillet and toss well to combine all the flavors.
  4. Season with salt, sprinkle chopped parsley and, if using, grated Parmesan cheese. Serve immediately.
